some sample queries you can try:

  • dog playing with a ball in the park
  • cutting vegetables
  • cooking vegetables
  • buying fruit
  • eating pizza at home

1. Kindly enter your query in the text box and click the Search button

2. Since it takes ~3 hours to generate LIME explanations on a GPU for each query, we have precached LIME explanations for 15 queries, for their top 10 results. These LIME enabled queries are shown in the dropbox when the user starts typing.

3. LIME explanations contain heavy payloads and may take several seconds to load. We REALLY appreciate your patience and support.
